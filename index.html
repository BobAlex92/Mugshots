Our Story

In a town that was once known for its legendary old jailhouse, Mugshots Coffeehouse has become a quirky hotspot for anyone looking to “do hard time” with a great cup of coffee. Owned by a history buff with a passion for the Wild West, the café is designed to give visitors a taste of the frontier days—complete with rustic wood furnishings, a vintage saloon bar for ordering , and walls lined with “wanted posters” of famous outlaws, sheriffs, and vigilantes (and a few familiar faces from around town).
